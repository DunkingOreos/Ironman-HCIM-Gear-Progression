DunkingOreos & Bear's Ironman Gear Progression — November 2025

This gear progression was put together by DunkingOreos and Bear. Users can track their progress by left clicking the item when you obtain it. For more information on the item itself you can right click and select "View on OSRS Wiki" and for tips or recommendations select the "View Tips" option.

Disclaimer: We understand putting together a gear progression is subjective to a point, some people can do Inferno at level 37 without needing overheads, some can beat the Colosseum without taking a single hit of damage, but we believe this progression set to be optimized and efficient for the average player.
